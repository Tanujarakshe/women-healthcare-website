Women's Healthcare

Access to proper healthcare is a fundamental right. Here are some important health tips:

  • Schedule annual health check-ups and screenings (e.g., mammograms, pap smears).
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise regularly to support physical and mental health.
  • Prioritize mental health through counseling or stress management techniques.
  • Stay informed about reproductive health and safe practices.
  • Get vaccinated for common preventable diseases.

Tips for Tracking Menstrual Cycle

Tracking your menstrual cycle can help you understand your body better and identify any potential health issues early. Here are some helpful tips:

  • Use a period tracking app like Clue, Flo, or My Calendar to easily log your cycle.
  • Mark the start and end dates of your period on a physical or digital calendar.
  • Track symptoms like cramps, mood changes, or headaches for better health awareness.
  • Note down flow intensity (light, medium, heavy) for each day.
  • Stay consistent by updating your records every cycle.
  • Consult a healthcare provider if you notice any irregularities.
